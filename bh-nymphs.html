Beadhead Nymphs

About Beadhead Nymphs

Beadhead nymphs revolutionized subsurface fly fishing by providing built-in weight at the head of the fly, allowing patterns to sink quickly into the strike zone where trout feed. The metal bead, typically brass or tungsten, adds both weight and flash that can trigger strikes from opportunistic fish. These versatile patterns are essential for any nymph fishing situation, from pocket water to deep pools.

Our beadhead collection features classic patterns enhanced with quality metal beads. The BH Pheasant Tail adds weight and attraction to Frank Sawyer's legendary mayfly imitation. The BH Hare's Ear sinks deeper while maintaining its buggy, lifelike appearance that trout find irresistible. The BH Prince Nymph combines the original pattern's effectiveness with improved sink rate for faster, deeper water. Each beadhead nymph is tied with properly sized brass beads that complement the fly's proportions while providing optimal weight for various water conditions.
