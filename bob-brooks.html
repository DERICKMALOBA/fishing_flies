About Bob Brooks Patterns

Bob Brooks is a renowned fly tier whose innovative patterns have earned respect throughout the fly fishing community. His designs combine traditional tying techniques with modern materials and fresh approaches, resulting in flies that consistently catch fish in challenging conditions. Each pattern reflects decades of on-stream observation and tying bench experimentation.

Our Bob Brooks collection features his signature patterns tied to exacting specifications. These flies represent some of the most creative and effective designs in modern fly tying, incorporating unique materials and construction techniques that set them apart from standard patterns. Whether you're fishing technical spring creeks or fast-flowing freestone rivers, Bob Brooks patterns deliver results when other flies fail to produce.
