Skip Morris Patterns

About Skip Morris Patterns

Skip Morris is a legendary figure in fly fishing, known both as an accomplished fly tier and acclaimed author of numerous fly fishing books. His patterns reflect decades of fishing experience and a deep understanding of what makes flies effective. Skip's designs are characterized by their practicality, durability, and fish-catching ability across a wide range of conditions.

Our Skip Morris collection includes his most celebrated patterns, each designed to be easily tied yet devastatingly effective. From his emerger designs to his innovative nymph patterns, these flies represent the work of a true master who has shared his knowledge with generations of anglers. Each pattern is tied following Skip's original recipes to ensure you get the same fish-catching performance that has made his flies famous.
