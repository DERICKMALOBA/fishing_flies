Czech Nymphs

About Czech Nymphs

Czech nymphs originated from competition fly fishing in the Czech Republic and Poland, where anglers developed highly effective short-line nymphing techniques that have since spread worldwide. These patterns are characterized by their curved hooks, weighted bodies, and slim profiles that sink rapidly and drift naturally along the stream bottom. The technique and the flies that accompany it have revolutionized subsurface fly fishing.

Our Czech nymph collection features authentic patterns tied in the European tradition. The Czech Nymph Classic with its segmented body and shellback imitates a variety of caddis larvae and aquatic worms. The Vladi Worm is a simple yet deadly pattern that has won countless competition medals. Each fly is tied on quality curved-shank hooks with proper weight distribution for that distinctive Czech nymph action. Whether you're competing in a tournament or simply want to catch more fish, these patterns will improve your nymphing success dramatically.
