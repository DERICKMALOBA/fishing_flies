Beadhead Streamers

About Beadhead Streamers

Beadhead streamers combine the fish-attracting action of traditional streamer patterns with the quick-sinking properties of weighted heads. The added bead not only helps the fly get down to where big fish lurk in deeper pools and undercut banks, but also creates an enticing jigging action when stripped. These patterns are essential when fishing fast or deep water where unweighted streamers can't reach the strike zone effectively.

Our beadhead streamer collection includes proven fish-catchers with brass and tungsten beads for various sink rates. The BH Woolly Bugger adds weight to an already legendary pattern, making it even more versatile for probing deep runs and pools. The BH Zonker's rabbit strip maintains its undulating motion while the bead drives the fly into the feeding zone. Each pattern is tied with durable materials on strong hooks designed to handle the aggressive strikes and powerful runs of large trout and other gamefish.
