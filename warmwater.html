Warmwater Flies

About Warmwater Flies

Warmwater flies are designed specifically for bass, panfish, carp, and other species found in lakes, ponds, and slow-moving rivers. These patterns typically feature bold colors, larger hooks, and durable construction to withstand the aggressive strikes of predatory warmwater fish. Fly fishing for bass and panfish offers exciting action and is increasingly popular among anglers looking to expand beyond traditional trout fishing.

Our warmwater collection includes proven patterns for targeting a variety of species. Bass buggers combine the action of marabou with the durability needed for toothy fish. Crayfish patterns imitate one of the most important food sources for largemouth and smallmouth bass. Our panfish flies feature smaller hooks and bright colors that bluegill and crappie can't resist. Each pattern is tied on strong hooks with materials selected for maximum durability and fish-catching action.
