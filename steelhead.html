Steelhead Flies

About Steelhead Flies

Steelhead flies are designed for one of fly fishing's greatest challenges - the mighty steelhead. These ocean-run rainbow trout are known for their power, stamina, and sometimes finicky behavior. Steelhead flies range from bright, flashy patterns that trigger aggressive strikes to subtle, natural presentations for pressured fish. Success often depends on having a variety of patterns to match changing conditions and fish moods.

Our steelhead collection includes proven patterns for both swinging and nymphing techniques. The Egg Sucking Leech is a Pacific Northwest classic that combines the attraction of a bright egg with the movement of a leech. Intruder-style flies create massive profiles and incredible movement in the water. Our egg patterns and nymphs cover dead-drift techniques for when steelhead are less aggressive. Each fly is tied on quality steelhead hooks with durable materials designed to handle the violent strikes and powerful runs these incredible fish deliver.
