Shane Stalcup Patterns

About Shane Stalcup Patterns

Shane Stalcup is celebrated for his innovative approach to imitating mayflies and caddis. His patterns emphasize realistic silhouettes and the use of modern synthetic materials that enhance durability while maintaining a natural appearance. Shane's flies have proven themselves on demanding waters where selective trout require accurate imitations.

Our Shane Stalcup collection showcases his most effective designs, particularly his groundbreaking work with CDC and synthetic wing materials. These patterns represent the cutting edge of mayfly and caddis imitation, offering anglers flies that fool even the most educated fish. Each pattern is tied following Shane's specifications to ensure proper proportions and fish-catching effectiveness.
