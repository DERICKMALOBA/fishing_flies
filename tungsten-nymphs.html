Tungsten Nymphs

About Tungsten Nymphs

Tungsten nymphs represent the cutting edge of modern nymph fishing technology, featuring beads made from tungsten rather than brass. Tungsten is approximately 1.7 times heavier than brass, allowing flies to sink faster and reach the strike zone more quickly in fast or deep water. This density advantage has made tungsten patterns the choice of competition anglers and serious nymph fishers worldwide.

Our tungsten collection includes patterns optimized for Euro nymphing and tight-line techniques. The Tungsten Jig Nymph features a slotted bead on a jig hook, riding hook-point-up to reduce snags while maintaining perfect drift. Perdigons are sleek, resin-coated patterns that cut through the water column like tiny bullets. Our Euro nymphs are tied on quality competition hooks with properly sized tungsten beads ranging from 2mm to 4mm, allowing you to match the weight to your water conditions. These patterns are essential for any angler looking to master the modern nymphing techniques that are catching more fish than ever before.
