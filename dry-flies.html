Dry Flies

About Dry Flies

Dry flies are the quintessential fly fishing pattern, designed to float on the water's surface and imitate adult insects. These elegant patterns represent the pinnacle of fly fishing artistry, requiring precise construction to achieve the perfect balance of buoyancy and natural presentation. When trout are actively feeding on the surface during a hatch, there's nothing more exciting than watching a fish rise to take your carefully presented dry fly.

Our collection includes time-tested classics like the Adams, one of the most versatile and effective dry flies ever created. The Elk Hair Caddis excels during caddis hatches with its distinctive silhouette and excellent floatability. Blue Wing Olives are essential for those overcast days when Baetis mayflies emerge in clouds. Each pattern in our dry fly selection is hand-tied with premium materials including high-quality hackle, CDC feathers, and durable hooks to ensure they float high and catch fish consistently. Whether you're fishing technical spring creeks or tumbling mountain streams, our dry flies will help you match the hatch and fool selective trout.
