Parachute Flies

About Parachute Flies

Parachute flies represent a revolutionary approach to dry fly design, featuring a horizontal hackle wrapped around a vertical post rather than the traditional vertical wrap. This innovative construction creates patterns that land softly, float in the surface film with a natural profile, and remain highly visible to anglers thanks to the prominent wing post. The parachute style has become indispensable for technical dry fly fishing where presentation and visibility are paramount.

The Adams Parachute has become perhaps the most popular variation of this legendary pattern, offering improved visibility while maintaining its incredible versatility. Our Purple Haze combines the parachute style with eye-catching colors that attract fish while remaining easy to track in complex currents. The Parachute BWO is essential for those cloudy days when Baetis mayflies emerge. Each parachute pattern in our collection is carefully tied to ensure the hackle is perfectly horizontal, providing optimal flotation and that distinctive low-riding presentation that fooling selective trout demands. The bright post materials we use make tracking your fly effortless, even in low light conditions.
