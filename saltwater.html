About Saltwater Flies

Saltwater fly fishing offers some of the most exciting angling experiences on the planet, from stalking bonefish on tropical flats to battling giant tarpon in coastal waters. Saltwater flies must be durable enough to withstand toothy predators, corrosive salt water, and powerful fish that test tackle to its limits. These patterns imitate shrimp, crabs, baitfish, and other prey that saltwater gamefish feed upon.

Our saltwater collection includes proven patterns for the most popular species. The Crazy Charlie is perhaps the most effective bonefish fly ever created, with its sparse profile and quick-sinking design. The Gotcha adds flash and is equally effective for spooky fish on bright days. Lefty's Deceiver, designed by legendary angler Lefty Kreh, is a versatile baitfish imitation that catches everything from stripers to tarpon. Each saltwater fly is tied on corrosion-resistant hooks with durable synthetic materials that hold up to the demands of salt water fishing.
