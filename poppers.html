About Popper Flies

Popper flies create surface disturbance that triggers explosive strikes from bass, panfish, and other predatory fish. The distinctive "pop" and gurgle these flies make when stripped across the surface imitates struggling baitfish or frogs, triggering aggressive predatory responses. There's nothing quite like watching a bass explode on a popper in the early morning or late evening hours.

Our popper collection includes foam, cork, and deer hair designs for various fishing situations. Foam poppers offer durability and consistent action, while cork poppers provide that classic look and feel. Deer hair poppers, though more time-consuming to tie, offer a softer landing and incredibly realistic action. Each popper is finished with quality hooks and rubber legs or feather tails that add movement and attraction. Whether you're targeting largemouth bass in lily pads or bluegill on a farm pond, our poppers deliver the topwater action you're looking for.
